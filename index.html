🌍 WorldSignal

WorldSignal is a global news and alert application that helps users track important events and crises around the world in real time using an interactive map.

The app focuses primarily on breaking news and global incidents. Users can also choose to follow additional categories such as sports, music, and other topics based on their preferences.
